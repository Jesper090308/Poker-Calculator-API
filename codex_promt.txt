Here are the most impactful improvements roughly in order of value:

**Equity Estimation**
- **Improved range weighting** — right now opponent ranges are static profiles. Updating them based on betting history across streets would be more realistic.
- **Street-aware sampling** — weight runouts by card removal effects (e.g. if you hold Ah, the flush completing on the river is less likely).
- **Equity buckets** — instead of recalculating equity from scratch every request, precompute equity for common hand/board combinations and cache them.

**Decision Making**
- **SPR awareness** (Stack-to-Pot Ratio) — the optimal strategy changes dramatically based on SPR. A 2x SPR spot plays very differently from a 10x SPR spot and your scorer doesn't account for this explicitly.
- **Nut advantage** — track who has more strong hands on a given board texture and adjust aggression accordingly.
- **Multi-street planning** — right now decisions are made one street at a time. Knowing "if I bet flop, what's my plan for turn/river?" produces better sizing.

**Opponent Modelling**
- **Bet sizing tells** — opponents who always bet small have air, large means value. Use bet sizing as an additional signal beyond just action type.
- **Dynamic profile updating** — start with a prior (unknown) and shift toward tighter/looser based on observed actions within the session.

**Performance**
- **LRU cache for equity lookups** — same hand + board + profile shouldn't be recomputed.
- **Early termination** — if equity is extremely high (>85%) or low (<15%), stop simulating early and just jam or fold.
